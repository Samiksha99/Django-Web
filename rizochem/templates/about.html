Who Are We

Rizochem International (Pharmaceutical)

We are a Global Trading House specialised in sourcing and exporting a ultimate range of products and services worldwide. We have a highly skilled team with advance technologies regulatory affairs specialists to deliver the best quality products at highly competitive prices in fast and easy trading process internationally.

Rizochem is one of the Fastest growing Pharma company in India. Our core strength lies in our ability to excel in developing over-the-counter and technologically complex medicines, at very low costs and without compromising on quality.

We have ensured world-class quality in design, equipment and operations in all our manufacturing facilities. Our global quality management team ensures that every product manufactured and distributed by us complies with all internationally accepted good and standards of quality, purity, efficacy and safety.

We are sure that with our unique business model, corporate values & with the culture of quality & excellence we shall lead our relationships with alliances and ultimately pave our way to newer heights.

Manufacturing

RIZOCHEM PHARMACEUTICALS is in third party manufacturing, all our manufacturing facilities have ensured world- class quality in design equipment and operations. Our all manufacturers ensure that we are able to provide best-in-class quality and economical pharmaceutical and nutraceutical products.

Our manufacturers are focused on producing speciality in Allopathic medicine, Nutraceutical, Herbal and Ayurvedic medicine in the full range of dosage forms, we have Medicinal tablets, capsules, injectables, ointments, Gynecological medicaments, Intimate Hygiene products, also have expertise in Effervescent tablets, injection, Amoxiclave, Cephalosporin, Nasal Spray, Eye Drops, Ointment, Cream, Gel, Lotions, Topical spray, Vaginal tablet ( Suppository ), Antibiotics, Antibacterial, Anti Viral, Anti cold, NSAID, Anti Histaminic, Antacid, Anti Fungal, Anti Scabies, Topical Steroids, Anti Acne, Skin lightning, Cough Syrup, Paediatric Preparation and Eye lubricant products.

Rizochem International (Pharmaceutical) has collaboration with only those manufacturing units which are based upon state of art manufacturing technologies approved by WHO-GMP, ISO and other various authority..
